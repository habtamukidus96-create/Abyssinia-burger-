Our Story

At Abyssinia Burger, we marry the bold, historic flavors of East Africa with the timeless tradition of the classic American gourmet burger.

Every single patty is crafted from premium, locally-sourced cuts of beef, seasoned to perfection, and paired with house-made sauces that you won't find anywhere else. We believe in rich flavors, fresh ingredients, and an unforgettable dining experience.
